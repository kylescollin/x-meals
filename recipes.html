Soup · 6 hrs
Slow Cooker Chili
6 hrs 15 min · Slow Cooker · Serves 8
Recipe ▾
Ingredients
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 large yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 2 (14.5 oz) cans diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 3 (8 oz) cans tomato sauce
  • ½ cup beef broth
  • 2 Tbsp chili powder
  • 2½ tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 (15 oz) can dark red kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15 oz) can light red kidney beans, drained
  • Shredded cheddar cheese for serving
Directions
  1. 1Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Sauté onion 3 min, add garlic 30 sec. Pour into slow cooker.
  2. 2Return skillet to medium-high. Add beef and cook, stirring occasionally, until browned. Drain most fat.
  3. 3Add beef to slow cooker. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, broth, all spices, salt and pepper.
  4. 4Cover and cook on low 5–6 hours.
  5. 5Stir in kidney beans and heat through, about 2 min. Serve with desired toppings.
💡 Look for tomatoes labeled "mild" green chilies. Bloom spices with aromatics in the last minute of sautéing for extra depth.

Pasta · 40 min
Creamy Sausage and Peppers Pasta
40 min · Stovetop · Serves 4
Recipe ▾
Ingredients
  • 8 oz uncooked pasta (bucatini recommended)
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • ½ medium onion, chopped
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach
  • Salt, pepper, freshly grated Parmesan to taste
Directions
  1. 1Boil salted pasta water and cook pasta al dente per package.
  2. 2Brown sausage in skillet over medium-high, breaking it up, 8–10 min. Transfer to paper towel–lined plate.
  3. 3Add oil, onion, and peppers to same skillet. Cook until onion is lightly browned, 7–8 min.
  4. 4Reduce to medium. Add butter and garlic; once melted, sprinkle in flour and cook 1 min.
  5. 5Stir in cream and Dijon — no lumps. Sauce thickens quickly; don't overcook.
  6. 6Add sausage and spinach. Splash in pasta water, drain pasta, add to skillet. Toss until spinach wilts.

Vegetarian Quesadillas
25 min · Stovetop · Serves 4
Recipe ▾
Ingredients
  • 4 medium flour tortillas
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 2 avocados
  • ½ cup black beans, rinsed
  • ¼ cup corn, rinsed
  • 1 mini red pepper
  • 1 mini orange pepper
  • 1 tsp jalapeno, diced (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1 cup cheddar or pepper jack cheese
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp olive oil, divided
  • Cilantro, lime, salsa, sour cream to serve

Butternut Squash Ravioli with Brown Butter Sage
8 min · Stovetop · Serves 4
Recipe ▾
Ingredients
  • 8–10 oz butternut squash ravioli
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cubed
  • 15 fresh sage leaves
  • 2 Tbsp heavy cream
  • ½ tsp kosher salt
  • ¼ tsp fresh cracked black pepper
  • Grated pecorino or Parmesan for serving
Directions
  1. 1Boil ravioli per package. Drain al dente; do not rinse.
  2. 2Melt butter in skillet over medium, stirring until it bubbles, then froths. Add sage — it will fry in the butter. Cook until froth subsides and brown bits form. Remove from heat. Transfer sage to paper towel.

  4. 4Add ravioli to sauce and toss. Serve with Parmesan and crispy sage.
💡 Total butter browning time: 4–5 minutes. Watch carefully — it goes from golden to burnt quickly.

Pasta · 1 hr 55 min
Best Lasagna
1 hr 55 min · Oven · Serves 12
Recipe ▾
Ingredients
  • 1 lb dried lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef (or turkey, or ½ beef ½ mild sausage)
  • 1 medium yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • ¼ cup tomato paste
  • 2 (28 oz) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 1½ tsp kosher salt, ¾ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 2 tsp sugar
  • 16 oz whole milk ricotta
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan, plus extra
  • 1 Tbsp dried parsley
  • 6 cups grated mozzarella
Directions
  1. 1Preheat oven to 375°F. Boil salted water; cook noodles al dente ~8 min. Drain, rinse, separate.
  2. 2Brown beef with onion and garlic over medium-high, ~8 min. Stir in tomato paste, 1 min. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper, seasoning, sugar. Simmer covered 15–20 min.
  3. 3Mix ricotta, egg, ¾ cup Parmesan, remaining salt and pepper.
  4. 4In 9×13 dish: 1 cup sauce, noodles, 1½ cups sauce, ¼ ricotta mix, 1¼ cups mozzarella. Repeat 3 more times. Top with remaining sauce, mozzarella, Parmesan.
  5. 5Cover loosely with foil. Bake 30 min. Uncover, bake 20 more min. Rest 5–10 min before serving.

20 min · Air Fryer · Serves 4
Recipe ▾
Ingredients
  • 1½ lb tilapia or white fish
  • 1 tsp chili powder, ½ tsp oregano, ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika, ¼ tsp cayenne, ¼ tsp onion powder
  • ¼ tsp cumin, ½ tsp sea salt, ½ tsp cracked pepper
  • Tortillas for serving
  • Slaw: 2½ cups coleslaw mix
  • 3 Tbsp mayo, 1 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1 garlic clove minced, ⅓ cup cilantro
  • ½ tsp sugar (optional), salt and pepper

  • 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, 2 tsp fresh ginger

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 8 oz crimini mushrooms, sliced
  • 8 oz spinach
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ¼ tsp salt, ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 9 oz refrigerated 3-cheese tortellini
Directions
  1. 1Caramelize onions: heat 2 Tbsp oil over medium-high; cook onions 10 min stirring constantly. Reduce to medium, cook 10 more min. Add pinch of salt, cook 10 min on medium-low. Total: 30 min. Remove to a plate.
