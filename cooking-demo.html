Meal A · Tuesday 3/17
Ground Turkey Teriyaki Stir Fry
🕒 25 min🍳 Stovetop👨‍👩‍👦 Serves 4
Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 2 cups brown rice
  • 1 head broccoli, florets
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil
Instructions
  1. 1Cook brown rice first — it takes longest and can rest while you cook everything else.
  2. 2Whisk soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, cornstarch, and ¼ cup water together. Set aside.
  3. 3Heat avocado oil in a large wok over medium-high. Add turkey and cook 5–6 min, breaking it up, until no longer pink.
  4. 4Push turkey to the side. Add broccoli, carrots, and bell pepper. Stir-fry 3–4 min until crisp-tender.
  5. 5Add garlic and ginger. Stir constantly 30 seconds until fragrant.
  6. 6Pour sauce over everything. Toss to coat and cook 1–2 min until glossy and thickened.
  7. 7Serve over rice. Top with green onions and sesame seeds.
Meal B · Wednesday 3/18
Creamy Tomato Gnocchi with Burrata
🕒 30 min🍳 Stovetop👨‍👩‍👦 Serves 4
Ingredients
  • 1 lb potato gnocchi
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 balls fresh burrata
  • 3 cups baby spinach
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil to serve
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Instructions
  1. 1Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium. Add garlic and cook 1 min — don't let it brown.
  2. 2Pour in crushed tomatoes and Italian seasoning. Simmer uncovered 10 min, stirring occasionally.
  3. 3Stir in cream. Season with salt and pepper. Cook 2 more minutes.
  4. 4Cook gnocchi per package in salted boiling water. Drain and add directly to the sauce.
  5. 5Stir in spinach and cook 1 min until just wilted. Tear burrata over the top and finish with fresh basil.
